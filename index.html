The Architect

Meet the Founder

Akira Hoshin
Founder & Chief Executive Officer
DiviDev — Parent Company of Vision Farm

Akira Hoshin is widely regarded as one of the most forward-thinking technology architects of his generation, known for designing large-scale innovation ecosystems that connect artificial intelligence, capital formation, and distributed computing infrastructure. Operating with a disciplined, systems-level approach to problem solving, Hoshin has built his reputation not through publicity, but through the creation of technologies and venture platforms that quietly shape emerging markets.

Born into a multicultural lineage that blended Japanese discipline with a global cultural perspective, Akira demonstrated exceptional analytical ability early in life, developing an unusual capacity for pattern recognition, strategic modeling, and advanced computational thinking. By his early years in the technology sector, he had already contributed to experimental distributed systems, autonomous software frameworks, and predictive intelligence tools that reflected a long-horizon view of how artificial intelligence would transform global industry.

Hoshin later founded DiviDev (Divine Developments), an advanced innovation company dedicated to incubating foundational technologies and launching high-impact ventures across artificial intelligence, infrastructure intelligence, and global digital economies. As the parent company behind multiple technology initiatives, DiviDev serves as the originating research, development, and commercialization engine responsible for bringing new systems to market.

Among DiviDev's flagship ventures is Vision Farm, a next-generation venture incubation platform designed to identify, build, and scale transformative artificial intelligence companies. Under Hoshin's leadership, Vision Farm operates as a strategic growth environment where emerging technologies are developed into scalable enterprises supported by capital, infrastructure, and advanced technical guidance. The platform reflects his long-standing philosophy that the future of innovation belongs to organizations capable of combining research, venture strategy, and technological execution within a single integrated ecosystem.

"The future belongs to those who can see what doesn't yet exist — and have the systems to bring it into being."

Despite his growing influence across the global technology landscape, Akira Hoshin remains known for his disciplined privacy and quiet operational style, focusing his public presence primarily on strategic insights, institutional partnerships, and long-term technological forecasting. As Founder and CEO of DiviDev and the strategic architect behind Vision Farm, his mission continues to center on building the foundational systems, venture platforms, and intelligence infrastructure that will power the next era of global technological development.
